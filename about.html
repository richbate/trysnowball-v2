Why Snowball Works

This tool was created by someone who’s lived with debt, struggled with impulse spending, and knows the power of seeing a way out. This page explains the logic behind the app and how you can make it work for you.

What the Numbers Mean

  • Total Debts: The full amount you owe. It’s the mountain we’re going to melt.
  • Interest Paid: Money lost to lenders. Our mission is to keep this as low as possible.
  • Repayments: The monthly total you’re already paying — it forms the base of your snowball.
  • Snowball: Your total repayment amount plus any extra you can afford. The bigger the snowball, the faster you knock down your debts.

Smart Strategies, Not Shame

This app doesn’t tell you what to cut or guilt you into frugality. Instead, it gives you data, clarity, and structure.

  • Track your spending. Whether you use Monzo, Snoop, or a notebook, knowing where your money goes changes everything.
  • Spot impulse patterns. Emotional spending is real — awareness is your superpower.
  • Commit to small increases. An extra £10/month could shave months off your debt journey.
  • Focus on your why. What would it mean to be debt-free? Security, peace, freedom? Write it down.

You’re Not Alone

This isn’t just math — it’s momentum. Every pound you throw at your snowball brings you closer to being free. You’ve got this.
